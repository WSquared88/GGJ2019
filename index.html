What Does HOME Mean To You?

Turn the real estate market on its head as the house hunter becomes the house hunted.

Play as a giant sentient house and gather room attachments to attract potential buyers. Keep your potential buyers enticed with new upgrades and features before planting your foundation and trapping everyone in a shoddily built and overpriced pile of bricks.

Build yourself up. Knock yourself down. Run around and hurt people (financially).

Gather more things to increase how much people like you. This model is accurate to real life.

Get those phat stacks. If you take a tumble, you'll lose progress on becoming the ultimate McMansion.
